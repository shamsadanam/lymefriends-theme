Changing the literacy and vocabulary of medicine

The future of healthcare depends on our ability to group together with others who are just like us, physically, mentally, emotionally, and structurally. Our power lies in understanding we are all biologically different and we each heal or becomes sick in different ways. Lymefriends hosts a platform for you to meet others uniquely similar to you: like online dating for wellness.

Lymefriends will change the literacy and vocabulary of medicine by democratizing health information for all to access freely. We use the wisdom of the crowd to democratize healthcare without discrimination in modalities of healing.

Symptoms, Not Diagnosis

Lymefriends exists on the premise that there are a finite number of symptoms, but an infinite number of diagnoses. This reduces or totally removes silos created when we are separated by disease type or a single diagnosis.

At Lymefriends, we believe removing these silos allows for faster symptom resolution, smarter interactions, and quicker sharing of information across disease or diagnosis groups. Activity happens within the security of an open and safe community built on empowerment and forward momentum. Our hope is to each find a way to continually become one day better each and every day.

Body

We are each affected differently by our symptoms. Some have only physical implications. These focus on our body: aches, pains, scrapes, bruises; cuts. Whether chronic or acute, physical injuries, just like mental or emotional injuries, can cross into our Mind or Spirit.

Mind

This is why, at Lymefriends, we like to categorize all symptoms as possibly being one, two, or all three of these affected areas. An old sports injury can begin to weigh on the mind. A mental injury can feel heavy on the spirit. Some symptoms are too complex to explain in simple terms.

Spirit

These sorts of symptoms can only be explained as spirit. We've found this to be most true of chronic symptoms. For example, chronic pain can become more complex over time; more difficult to both describe and treat. Our system helps you pair with other people whose symptoms feel like your own.

About Lymefriends

Lymefriends offers hope to the millions of people suffering from Lyme and symptoms. We provide a free, easy-to-use online platform to help people track and chart daily to gain a better understanding of their health and how behaviors, choices, and lifestyles have an impact on health.
